How It Works

This is not a “plug-and-pray” chatbot. We set your agent up like a real business operator — with rules, tone, and routing that match how you actually sell and deliver.

  • 1) Onboarding (10 minutes): You answer a short form about your offers, FAQs, and preferred next steps.
  • 2) Agent Build: We configure the agent’s tone, questions, qualification logic, and routing rules.
  • 3) Deployment: We deploy it to your website or inbox and connect lead capture to your email/CRM.
  • 4) Optimization: Every month we refine messaging, improve routing, and adjust flows based on what’s working.

Result: faster response times, cleaner lead handling, and fewer missed opportunities — without you living on your phone.
